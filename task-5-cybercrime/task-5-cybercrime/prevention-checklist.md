# Stay Safe Online — Prevention Checklist for College Students

1. ✅ Never click on links in unsolicited emails or SMS messages
2. ✅ Always check the URL before entering login details — look for HTTPS
3. ✅ Use two-factor authentication (2FA) on all important accounts
4. ✅ Don't share OTPs with anyone — including people claiming to be bank officials
5. ✅ 💰 Never save your UPI PIN or card details on shared devices
6. ✅ 💰 Set a daily UPI transaction limit in your banking app
7. ✅ Keep your phone and apps updated to get latest security patches
8. ✅ Use a password manager and avoid reusing passwords across platforms

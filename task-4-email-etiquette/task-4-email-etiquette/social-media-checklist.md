# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## ✅ DO's
1. Keep your profiles professional and accurate
2. Think before you post — once online, always online
3. Use strong unique passwords for each platform
4. Report harassment or abusive content immediately
5. Give credit when sharing others' work

## ❌ DON'Ts
1. Don't share personal info like phone number publicly
2. Don't post during exams — it reflects poorly
3. Don't engage with strangers asking for money
4. Don't spread unverified news or forwards
5. Don't use the same password across platforms
